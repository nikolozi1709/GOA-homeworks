OUR STORY

Started with one dog
nobody could place.

FURY began when a local shelter ran out of time for a senior dog named Biscuit. We built a simple way for shelters to post, and for people nearby to actually see. Years later, FURY connects dozens of shelters with thousands of adopters every month — and Biscuit is still very much sitting on someone's couch.
